2ï¸âƒ£  TF-IDF COSINE SIMILARITY
    What: Text mining approach
    How: Treats each skill as a word, measures importance
    Handles: Skill frequency & importance automatically
    Result: 0-100 score

3ï¸âƒ£  VECTOR COSINE SIMILARITY
    What: Binary vector approach
    Formula: dot_product / (||user|| * ||role||)
    Handles: Similarity between vectors
    Result: 0-100 score

4ï¸âƒ£  FREQUENCY WEIGHTING
    What: Skills appearing multiple times get higher weight

    Result: Weighted match score

5ï¸âƒ£  FUZZY MATCHING
